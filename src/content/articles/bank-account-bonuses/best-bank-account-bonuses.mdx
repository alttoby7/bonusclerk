## Common Mistakes to Avoid

<Callout type="warning" title="Direct Deposit Confusion">
Not every ACH transfer counts as a direct deposit. Payroll always works, but P2P transfers (Venmo, Zelle, PayPal) almost never do. Always [check DD compatibility](/dd-checker) before relying on a non-payroll method.
</Callout>

1. **Missing the deadline.** Most bonuses expire 60-90 days after opening. Set calendar reminders on day 1.
2. **Closing too early.** Banks charge $25-50 early termination fees if you close within 6-12 months.
3. **Wrong transfer type.** An ACH push that doesn't code as "direct deposit" means wasted time and no bonus.
4. **ChexSystems flags.** Opening 4+ new accounts in a short period can trigger denials. Space applications out.
5. **Forgetting about taxes.** Your bank will send a 1099-INT. Budget 24-37% of the bonus for taxes depending on your bracket.
