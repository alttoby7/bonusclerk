## Current Offer

$400 is on the table from U.S. Bank's Smartly Checking promotion, structured as a 2-tier bonus: $200 for $3,000–$4,999.99 in qualifying direct deposits within 90 days of account opening, or $400 for $5,000+ in qualifying direct deposits within 90 days. The offer expires April 30, 2026. Verified Apr 6, 2026.

The structural advantage of this bonus is the built-in fee waiver. Smartly Checking carries a $12/mo monthly fee drag, but U.S. Bank waives it when combined monthly direct deposits reach $1,500 in a given month. The $400 tier requires $5,000+ over 90 days -- even spread across three months that averages $1,667/mo, which clears the $1,500 fee waiver threshold. The $200 tier ($3,000 total) auto-waives fees only if DDs are concentrated in one or two months rather than spread evenly across all 90 days.

No promo code is required. The $25 opening deposit is the only upfront capital lockup. U.S. Bank does not charge an early termination fee. The bonus posts within 30 days after the month-end when the requirement stack is met, and the account must remain open with a positive balance at the time the bonus credits.

## Requirement Stack

1. **Open account online or in-branch** -- $25 minimum deposit, no promo code needed.
2. **Enroll in the U.S. Bank Mobile App or online banking** within 90 days of account opening.
3. **Set up 2+ qualifying direct deposits within 90 days** -- totaling $3,000–$4,999.99 for the $200 tier, or $5,000+ for the $400 tier. The deposits can span multiple pay cycles within that window.
4. **Wait for the bonus** -- arrives within 30 days after the end of the qualifying month.

<Callout type="tip" title="90-Day Clock">
The 90-day clock starts at account opening. Qualifying direct deposits must total the threshold within 90 days of opening. Deposits can span multiple pay cycles -- they do not need to fall in a single calendar month. If you open April 15, the 90-day window closes July 14.
</Callout>

## Fee Math

`$400 bonus - $0 monthly fee drag (waived) - $0 early termination fee = **$400 net bonus after fees**`
