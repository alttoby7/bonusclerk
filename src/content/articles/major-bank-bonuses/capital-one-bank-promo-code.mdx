## Requirement Stack

**OFFER300 / GET400 path:** Two qualifying direct deposits of $500 or more each, both landing within 75 days of account opening.

Capital One's official direct deposit definition: "a regular periodic payment such as salary, pension, or government benefits (Social Security) from an employer or government via ACH or RTP." Zelle transfers do not count. Person-to-person payments do not count.

Community-reported data shows that ACH pushes from brokerages and fintech platforms often trigger the requirement. This is not bank-confirmed -- see the DD Evidence section below for 21 tested sources.

**DEBIT250 path:** 20 debit card purchases of $10 or more within 75 days. No direct deposit of any kind is needed. Each transaction must post (not just authorize) within the window.

**BONUS1500 savings path:** Deposit $20k, $50k, or $100k into a new 360 Performance Savings account within 15 days of opening, then hold that balance for 90 days. The corresponding bonus is $300, $750, or $1,500. Transfers from another Capital One account do not qualify. The tier downgrade rule applies -- if your balance drops below a tier threshold during the 90-day hold, you receive the lower tier bonus.

## Fee Math

Capital One 360 Checking charges $0 per month. No minimum balance. No monthly fee drag.

- **OFFER300:** $300 bonus - $0 fees = **$300 net**
- **GET400:** $400 bonus - $0 fees = **$400 net**
- **DEBIT250:** $250 bonus - $0 fees = **$250 net**
